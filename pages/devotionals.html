The Power of Persistent Prayer

5 min read Luke 18:1-8 Rev. Michael Thompson

In the parable of the persistent widow, Jesus teaches us about persevering in prayer. The widow's continual appeals to the unjust judge eventually led to justice. How much more will our loving Father answer His children who cry out to Him day and night?
